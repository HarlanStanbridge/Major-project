Introduction

Welcome to the user manual for our Hotel Booking system. This manual provides essential information to help you use our booking system effectively and efficiently.

Getting Started

To get started with our Hotel Booking system, follow the steps below:

  1. Visit our website and click on the "Booking" link in the navigation menu.
  2. Fill in all the required details in the booking form, including your first name, surname, check-in date, check-out date, room type, number of guests, and contact email.
  3. Click on the "Book" button to make your booking.
  4. If you haven't logged in yet, you'll be prompted to log in with your username and password. If you don't have an account, you can create one by clicking on the "Create Account" button.
  5. After logging in or creating an account, your booking will be confirmed, and you'll receive a booking confirmation email.
